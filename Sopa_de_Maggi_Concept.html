Sopa de Maggi Concept

Sopa de Maggi is a soup made from store-bought packets of noodle soup mix and is a staple in most Latin homes. Maggi is a versatile soup mix that can be used to make various recipes. My recipe is from versions of the soup I had when I was little.

Sopa de Maggi is a dish I've enjoyed my entire life and is a core memory I have of homemade meals. My grandmother would make it whenever she babysat me or whenever I was sick, it was served by itself as it only had noodles and flavoring in the packets but as I grew up I added more ingredients to it. I serve it with a piece of bread, usually Pan Frances and with a soda.
